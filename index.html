The History of Halloween

Halloween originated from the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain. Celts celebrated the end of the harvest season, believing spirits could walk among the living. Romans later merged their traditions, and with the influence of Christianity, Halloween evolved into the celebration we know today.

Why Do We Dress Up?

The tradition of costumes began as a way to ward off evil spirits by disguising oneself. Today, costumes are a fun way to express creativity, from spooky ghouls to pop culture icons!
